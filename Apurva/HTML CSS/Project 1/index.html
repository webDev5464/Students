Certainly! Cartoons are a form of animated entertainment that have been popular for decades, captivating audiences of all ages. Here's some general information about cartoons:

Definition: Cartoons are typically defined as illustrated or animated visual media that tell a story or convey a message through a sequence of images. They can be hand-drawn, computer-generated, or a combination of both.

Target Audience: Cartoons can be created for various target audiences, including children, teenagers, and adults. Some cartoons are specifically designed to entertain and educate young children, while others cater to older audiences with more complex themes and humor.
