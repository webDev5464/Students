The poem Problem

In the realm where clouds convene, A symphony of gray and serene, Comes a dance from the heavens above, As raindrops descend, a gift of love. Upon my face, they gently land, Like nature's kisses, soft and grand, Each droplet, a bead of liquid grace, Caressing my skin with a tender embrace. The pitter-patter on rooftops and leaves, A rhythmic melody that the rain weaves, It whispers secrets to the earth below, A language only nature's soul can know. Raindrops, like tears from the sky's eyes, Cleanse the world with a heartfelt sigh, Washing away sorrows, washing away pain, Renewing the spirit, like a gentle rain.

Solution

    In the realm where clouds convene,
    A symphony of gray and serene,
    Comes a dance from the heavens above,
    As raindrops descend, a gift of love.

    Upon my face, they gently land,
    Like nature's kisses, soft and grand,
    Each droplet, a bead of liquid grace,
    Caressing my skin with a tender embrace.

    The pitter-patter on rooftops and leaves,
    A rhythmic melody that the rain weaves,
    It whispers secrets to the earth below,
    A language only nature's soul can know.

    Raindrops, like tears from the sky's eyes,
    Cleanse the world with a heartfelt sigh,
    Washing away sorrows, washing away pain,
    Renewing the spirit, like a gentle rain.
